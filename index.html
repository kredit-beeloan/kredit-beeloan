What is an unsecured business loan?

An unsecured business loan is a type of loan that does not require the borrower to provide any collateral or security against the loan. Instead, the loan is granted based on the borrower's creditworthiness, financial history, and ability to repay the loan. As there is no collateral involved, unsecured business loans typically have higher interest rates than secured loans.
